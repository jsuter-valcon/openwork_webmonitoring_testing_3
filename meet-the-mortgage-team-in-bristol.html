Meet our mortgage team

Meet Our Dedicated UK-Based Mortgage Team for Expert Advice and Support

Our Experienced Mortgage Team working in Bristol, Swindon and across the UK

At Strong Mortgage Solutions, our dedicated mortgage team has been providing expert advice and assistance to clients for many years. With our extensive experience and in-depth knowledge of the mortgage industry, we are well-equipped to meet all your mortgage needs. Whether you are a first-time buyer, looking to remortgage, or interested in buy-to-let investments, our team is here to guide you every step of the way.


We understand that finding the right mortgage can be a complex and overwhelming process. That's why our team of qualified advisers is committed to offering personalised solutions tailored to your unique financial circumstances and goals. We take the time to listen to your needs, assess your options, and provide you with the best mortgage advice.


Our mortgage team operates across multiple locations, including Bristol, Swindon and across the UK. This allows us to serve clients throughout the UK, ensuring convenient access to our services. Whether you prefer face-to-face meetings or virtual consultations, our advisers are flexible and can accommodate your preferences.


To learn more about our team and their individual expertise, simply click on the profiles below. Each profile provides detailed information about our advisers, including their qualifications, experience, and areas of specialisation. You can also book an appointment directly through their personal links, making it easy to connect with the adviser who best suits your needs.


We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, supporting you not just during the mortgage process but also throughout your homeownership journey. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service, clear communication, and ongoing support.


Take the first step towards securing the right mortgage for your needs by reaching out to our experienced mortgage team. We are here to help you make informed decisions and navigate the mortgage market with confidence.


Contact us today to schedule an appointment and embark on your mortgage journey with a trusted and reliable team by your side.
